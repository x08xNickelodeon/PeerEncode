# PeerEncode

PeerEncode is a current working in progress project

Road map/General Idea

PeerEncode will be a mostly open source project that will allow people to share compute power to Transcode each other's files.

Simply Drop your files into the input. Start the program. The program will Automatically ask our server who is avalible for transcoding then it will chunk of your file and send it to their RIG tobe transcoded for you. In the mean time while your waiting for your file to be distubied across the network you will receive a chunk of someone else's video to transcode.

!!DISCLAIMER!!

It might be possible for people to see small bits of what your getting transcoded 

You will have to meet the hardware requirements before you are allowed to use the network 

Min Requirements:
- CPU: 4c/8t 2.5-3GHZ (at least 4th gen intel or zen 2 Ryzen)
- GPU: GTX 10xx is the min but would prefer GTX 16xx or Higher
- Ram: At least 1GB dedicated to the program
- Internet Connection: At least 250MB/s speed but 1gbs is prefered
- Bandwidth: Will use the same amount of bandwidth as your file's sizes with a chance of compression
